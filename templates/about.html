Meet Our Resource Person

Goodness C. Okwueze

Goodness Chimaobi Okwueze is a scrum master (servant leader), she also serves as a Personal Support Worker (PSW) in Canada, her current place of residence. She was born into a business-oriented family in Nigeria and is honored to be able to socialize with people of different ethnicity, religious orientation, and works of life, just like in Canada. This leadership attribute allows her to manage and advise all kinds of people. Goodness holds a Bachelor's degree in the field of Nursing Science.

As a growing child, Goodness had the privileged of learning the shared values of her parents in the area of financial responsibility. Being exposed to her dad's investments at the time helped her to understand the importance of investing in shares and stocks as her father would always fall back to them in challenging times.

Goodness' interest in the field of financial freedom, further propelled her into learning more from experts and world recognized organizations. Currently, she earns higher income while continuing her studies in this field, and is happy to work with families and individuals on the best ways to help them stabilize their finances.
